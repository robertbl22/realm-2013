Challenge

With the “We Love Logistics” campaign launch, adoption by UPS employees was critical to brand success. Unless those in daily contact with customers were transformed into brand ambassadors, the campaign would fail.

Solution

Concise, actionable communication was key. In our nearly 20 years partnering with UPS on their internal magazine, InsideUPS has consistently proven to be the best source for conveying information, influencing action and instilling pride. It’s become the most efficient and effective marketing tool – communicating with employees on paper, in their homes, on their time – for those interacting with customers daily and affecting UPS’s success directly.
