Realm Advertising, Inc. is committed to protecting your privacy and developing technology that gives you the most powerful and safe online experience. There is also information about your computer hardware and software that is automatically collected by Realm Advertising, Inc. This information can include: your IP address, browser type, domain names, access times and referring website addresses. This information is used by Realm Advertising, Inc. for the operation of the service, to maintain quality of the service, and to provide general statistics regarding use of the Realm Advertising, Inc. website.

Please keep in mind that if you directly disclose personally identifiable information or personally sensitive data through Realm Advertising, Inc. public message boards, this information may be collected and used by others. Note: Realm Advertising, Inc. does not read any of your private online communications.

Realm Advertising, Inc. encourages you to review the privacy statements of websites you choose to link to from Realm Advertising, Inc. so that you can understand how those websites collect, use and share your information. Realm Advertising, Inc. is not responsible for the privacy statements or other content on websites outside of the Realm Advertising, Inc. and Realm Advertising, Inc. family of websites.

Use of Your Personal Information

Realm Advertising, Inc. collects and uses your personal information to operate the Realm Advertising, Inc. website and deliver the services you have requested. Realm Advertising, Inc. also uses your personally identifiable information to inform you of other products or services available from Realm Advertising, Inc. and its affiliates. Realm Advertising, Inc. may also contact you via surveys to conduct research about your opinion of current services or of potential new services that may be offered.

Realm Advertising, Inc. does not sell, rent or lease its customer lists to third parties. Use of Cookies

The Realm Advertising, Inc. website uses “cookies” to help you personalize your online experience. A cookie is a text file that is placed on your hard disk by a Web page server. Cookies cannot be used to run programs or deliver viruses to your computer. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you, and can only be read by a Web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you.

One of the primary purposes of cookies is to provide a convenience feature to save you time. The purpose of a cookie is to tell the Web server that you have returned to a specific page. For example, if you personalize Realm Advertising, Inc. pages, or register with Realm Advertising, Inc. site or services, a cookie helps Realm Advertising, Inc. to recall your specific information on subsequent visits. This simplifies the process of recording your personal information, such as billing addresses, shipping addresses, and so on. When you return to the same Realm Advertising, Inc. website, the information you previously provided can be retrieved, so you can easily use the Realm Advertising, Inc. features that you customized.

You have the ability to accept or decline cookies. Most Web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser setting to decline cookies if you prefer. If you choose to decline cookies, you may not be able to fully experience the interactive features of the Realm Advertising, Inc. services or websites you visit.

Security of Your Personal Information

Realm Advertising, Inc. secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Realm Advertising, Inc. secures the personally identifiable information you provide on computer servers in a controlled, secure environment, protected from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. When personal information (such as a credit card number) is transmitted to other websites, it is protected through the use of encryption, such as the Secure Socket Layer (SSL) protocol.
