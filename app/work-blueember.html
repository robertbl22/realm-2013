Challenge

Fiesta’s new Blue Ember product included first-ever digital technology to control the gas grill’s temperature like an oven. But how do you get the attention of retailers and the media in a category that hasn’t changed in 20 years?

Solution

Create your own category. By defining all non-computerized grills as “conventional,” this allowed us to take Blue Ember beyond product introduction, defining it as the world’s first digital grill. The National Hardware Show became the launch site. Videos designed for a retailer’s in-store demonstration captured the lifestyles of our target audiences – everyday grillers, entertaining grillers, backyard chefs – highlighting convenience, simplicity and precision.

Services provided:

Audit: Discovery, Research, Marketing audit, Customer audit, Competitive audit/analysis, Audience profiles

Strategy: Brand positioning, Key messaging

Creative: Integrated marketing campaign, Product launch/promotion, Tradeshows, Video
